## Title: 1984
A dystopian novel set in a totalitarian society ruled by Big Brother, where surveillance and propaganda control every aspect of life. 
Winston Smith begins to question the system and secretly searches for truth and freedom. 
Themes include oppression, control, surveillance, truth, and resistance.

## Title: The Hobbit
A fantasy adventure about Bilbo Baggins, a quiet hobbit who is unexpectedly taken on a quest to reclaim a treasure guarded by a dragon. 
Along the journey, he faces danger, forms friendships, and discovers his inner courage. 
Themes include adventure, friendship, bravery, and self-discovery.

## Title: Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone
A young boy discovers he is a wizard and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. 
Harry makes friends and uncovers secrets about his past while facing dark forces. 
Themes include magic, friendship, courage, and the battle between good and evil.

## Title: The Lord of the Rings
An epic fantasy story about a group of heroes trying to destroy a powerful ring that could bring darkness to the world. 
Frodo and his companions face great dangers on their journey. 
Themes include friendship, sacrifice, good vs evil, and heroism.

## Title: To Kill a Mockingbird
A novel set in the American South that explores racial injustice through the eyes of a young girl named Scout. 
Her father, Atticus Finch, defends a black man falsely accused of a crime. 
Themes include justice, morality, racism, and empathy.

## Title: The Book Thief
Set in Nazi Germany, the story follows a young girl who finds comfort in stealing books and sharing them with others. 
Narrated by Death, the novel highlights the power of words during dark times. 
Themes include war, loss, friendship, and the power of storytelling.

## Title: All Quiet on the Western Front
A powerful story about soldiers in World War I and the harsh realities of life on the battlefield. 
It shows the emotional and physical toll of war on young men. 
Themes include war, trauma, loss, and the brutality of conflict.

## Title: Pride and Prejudice
A romantic novel that explores relationships, social class, and misunderstandings between Elizabeth Bennet and Mr. Darcy. 
It highlights personal growth and changing perceptions. 
Themes include love, society, class, and personal development.

## Title: The Alchemist
A philosophical story about a young shepherd named Santiago who follows his dreams in search of treasure. 
Along the way, he learns about destiny, purpose, and listening to his heart. 
Themes include destiny, dreams, self-discovery, and spirituality.

## Title: Brave New World
A futuristic dystopian novel where society is controlled through technology, conditioning, and pleasure rather than fear. 
Individuals are engineered and emotions are suppressed. 
Themes include control, technology, freedom, and individuality.

## Title: Dune
A science fiction story set on a desert planet where political intrigue, religion, and power struggles shape the future. 
Paul Atreides must navigate complex alliances and fulfill a greater destiny. 
Themes include power, politics, survival, destiny, and ecology.

## Title: The Little Prince
A poetic tale about a young prince who travels between planets, learning about human nature and relationships. 
The story is simple yet deeply philosophical. 
Themes include love, loneliness, innocence, and perspective on life.
